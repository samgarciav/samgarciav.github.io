Site Purpose

Keep The Weather (KTW) is a place not only for people interested in the climate but for those who are passionate about the environment and the planet. At KTW, we share a passion for the earth and its resources, understanding that its condition affects our daily lives. Users of the site share their interest in nature and their use of it.

The climate is an essential part of our lives, its state influences the way we experience, and its constant change makes every day different. At KTW, users can share the climate state at their local regions in real-time, as well as share stories, experiences, and ideas of how to take advantage of weather conditions for the benefit of the planet.
