Target Audience

The target audience is young adults between 20 - 40 years old. Man and Females passionate about the environment and willing to make a change favoring the planet. All those who like outdoor activities and take care of the weather. In the same way, those who like to share their actions and give ideas to others for the proper use and exploitation of the resources where the weather is involved.
